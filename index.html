Power will likely come from a naturally aspirated 2.4-litre flat-four engine as its US-only twin, producing 228bhp and 249Nm of torque. That’s 31bhp and 44Nm more than the previous GT86’s output, and buyers should be able to choose between a six-speed manual or a six-speed automatic gearbox.

Just a car everyone loves

The Toyota 86, also known as the GT86 in Europe and the Scion FR-S in the United States, is a sports car jointly developed by Toyota and Subaru. It features a front-engine, rear-wheel-drive layout and is powered by a 2.0L Subaru Boxer engine.

The design of the 86 pays homage to Toyota's past sports cars, with a sleek and aerodynamic body. The handling is precise and the driving experience is engaging, making it a popular choice among car enthusiasts.
